Featured Recipe: Chocolate Cake

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Flour 2 cups
Cocoa Powder 1 cup
Sugar 1.5 cups
Eggs 3 pcs

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix all dry ingredients.
  3. Add eggs and wet ingredients.
  4. Pour into a pan and bake for 30 minutes.
